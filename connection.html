How They Differ

The Second Great Awakening focused on emotional revival and personal conversion, while Unitarianism focused on reason and education.

How Unitarianism Influenced the Movement

Unitarianism criticized revival meetings for being too emotional. This created a clear contrast between emotional religion and rational religion in America.

Because of this disagreement, revivalists emphasized emotional preaching even more, strengthening the impact of the Second Great Awakening.
