How They Differ

The Second Great Awakening focused on emotional revival and personal conversion, while Unitarianism focused on reason and education.

How Unitarianism Influenced the Movement

Unitarianism criticized revival meetings for being too emotional. This created a clear divide between emotional religion and rational religion in America.

Because of this, revivalists in the Second Great Awakening emphasized emotional preaching even more strongly.
