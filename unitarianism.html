What is Unitarianism?

Unitarianism was a religious movement that focused on reason, education, and moral improvement instead of emotional revival experiences.

Thomas Jefferson

Influenced Unitarian ideas by promoting reason and a rational approach to religion.

John Adams

Supported religious tolerance and encouraged thinking based on logic rather than strict doctrine.

Millard Fillmore

Supported religious diversity and helped create a culture where Unitarian ideas could grow.
