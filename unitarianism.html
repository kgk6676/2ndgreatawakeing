Beliefs

Unitarianism was a religious movement mainly in New England. It rejected traditional ideas like the Trinity and focused more on reason and moral behavior.

Main Ideas

  • Religion should be based on reason
  • People improve through education and moral growth
  • Less focus on emotional religious experiences

View on Revivalism

Unitarians often criticized revival meetings because they believed religion should be calm, thoughtful, and focused on long-term moral development.
